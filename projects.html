Hunger Map Optimizer

Built for the Stanford CS109 Probability Challenge. A probabilistic model simulating a volunteer driving around a city to rescue leftover meals from restaurants before they spoil. Combines uncertain food amounts, spoilage times, and routing decisions into a single random variable to compare strategies (Greedy, Nearest, Random) and estimate which performs best on average. Addresses the intersection of food waste and food insecurity through probability modeling.
